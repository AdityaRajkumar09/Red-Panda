This website covers a detailed explanation on why these animals need to be saved and how you can help. The red pandas are racoon like creatures with reddish-brown fur. They are primarily found in the eastern himalayas. Like pandas, they mainly eat bamboo but as they are excellent climbers and extremely energetic they eat small birds, mammals, eggs, flowers and berries

The red pandas are critically endangered with a population of only 10,000. They suffer a cold hard winter due to deforestation and are killed for local cultural ceremonies, they are also poached for their fur, teeth and tail. The rarer they get the more they are being poached. This practice has been going on since the 13th century as depicted from a Chinese scroll
